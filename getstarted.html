Get Started on Your Urban Garden


Assess your resources

  • Identify what spaces you can allocate
  • Make a note of sun exposure and climate
  • Consider how much you can budget

Do your research

  • What kinds of plants interest you?
  • What kinds of plants seem likely to succeed, given your space, climate, and skill level?
  • How can you use the unique features of your space?

Make short-term and long-term goals

  • Start by considering what you already have. Anything that makes your space different can be an asset.
  • Not everything has to happen this season, or even this year!
  • Invest in a few good planters if you can, but repurposed containers can also get the job done.
    • Many of your plans and ideas won't turn out exactly as you imagined - some will turn out even better! Enjoy the endless opportunities to learn.
    Your space is unique! Don't be frustrated when you see cool projects that don't work in your space - be inspired.
